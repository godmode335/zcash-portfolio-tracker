If you hold Zcash for privacy, using a cloud portfolio tracker that wants your email and your wallet addresses is a step backwards. Here is how to track ZEC while staying private.

## Why not just paste your address?
Shielded Zcash (z-addresses) cannot be read from the public blockchain — that is the whole point. Only transparent t-addresses expose a balance. So address-based trackers either see nothing or expose your transparent activity.

## The private approach: record your own transactions
1. Note each buy and sell: amount, price, and date.
2. Enter them into a **local** tracker that computes your holdings and profit/loss.
3. Refresh prices from a public source — that request reveals only the coin name, not your balance.
