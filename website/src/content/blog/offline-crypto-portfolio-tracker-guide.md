An "offline" or local-first crypto portfolio tracker stores your data on your own device instead of a company's servers. For privacy-minded holders, it is the obvious choice.

## What "local-first" actually means
- Your holdings, transactions, and history live in a file on **your** PC.
- The app works without logging into any account.
- The only network calls are public price lookups — they never include your balances.

## What to look for
1. **Local storage** (a database file on disk), not a cloud sync.
2. **No account requirement.**
3. **Minimal network footprint** — prices only.
4. **Clear profit/loss** based on the transactions you enter.
