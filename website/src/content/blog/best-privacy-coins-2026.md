Privacy coins exist to do one thing mainstream cryptocurrencies cannot: keep your financial activity private. In 2026, a handful of projects lead the space, each with a different approach to confidentiality.

## Zcash (ZEC)
Zcash uses zero-knowledge proofs (zk-SNARKs) to offer **optional** shielded transactions. Sent between shielded addresses, the sender, receiver, and amount are all encrypted. Zcash is now the largest privacy coin by market capitalization.

## Monero (XMR)
Monero is private **by default** — ring signatures, stealth addresses, and confidential transactions hide every transfer automatically. It remains the benchmark for on-by-default privacy.

## Dash, Firo, and Zano
Dash offers optional, lighter privacy and is mainly a payments coin. Firo uses a burn-and-redeem model to break transaction history. Zano combines ring signatures with hybrid consensus and confidential assets.
